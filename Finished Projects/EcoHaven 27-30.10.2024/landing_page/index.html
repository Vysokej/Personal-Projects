5 Simple Swaps for a Greener Home

Transform your everyday routine with these easy and affordable eco-friendly swaps. From reusable kitchen essentials to sustainable bathroom products, discover how small changes can make a big impact on the planet.

Plastic Bags → Reusable Tote Bags:
Paper Towels → Cloth Towels
Plastic Wrap → Beeswax Wraps
Disposable Razors → Safety Razors
Plastic straws → Stainless steel straws
